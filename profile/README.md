## About

Tremblay Holdings is the parent entity of the Tremblay corporate ecosystem, founded by Julien Tremblay. It operates at the governance layer — allocating capital to subsidiaries, stewarding executive leadership, and ensuring all entities align with the core mission of recursive systems, dimensional intelligence, and innovative technology.
